Welcome to Baiano’s Menu!

Discover a delicious selection of authentic Brazilian flavors crafted with fresh ingredients and passion. Whether you are craving traditional classics or modern twists, our dishes are designed to delight your palate and satisfy your appetite. Explore our menu and find your new favorite today!

Moqueca Baiana

A fragrant seafood stew with fish, coconut milk, tomatoes, peppers, and dendê oil. Slow-cooked to perfection, this Bahia classic bursts with tropical, rich flavors.

Bobó de Camarão

Creamy shrimp stew made with mashed cassava, coconut milk, and dendê oil. A smooth, comforting dish with bold Afro-Brazilian flavors and tender shrimp.

Acarajé

Golden fried black-eyed pea fritters, crispy outside and soft inside, stuffed with spicy vatapá and shrimp. A popular street food from Bahia with rich, savory taste.

Abará

Steamed black-eyed pea dumplings wrapped in banana leaves, soft and flavorful. Served with vatapá or spicy sauces, it is a traditional Afro-Brazilian delicacy.

Caruru

Okra stew cooked with shrimp, palm oil, and nuts. This rich, savory dish offers a delightful texture and earthy flavors rooted in Afro-Brazilian tradition.

Xinxim de Galinha

Chicken stew slow-cooked with shrimp, peanuts, and palm oil. A hearty dish full of bold flavors and Afro-Brazilian roots.

Feijoada

A rich black bean stew with pork, sausage, and beef, served with rice, collard greens, and orange slices—a hearty Brazilian comfort food classic.

Pão de Queijo

Warm, chewy, and cheesy Brazilian cheese breads, perfect for snacking or as a side. Crispy outside, soft inside, made from tapioca flour and cheese.

Coxinha

Golden, crispy fried dough filled with shredded chicken. Shaped like a drumstick, it is a beloved Brazilian street food snack packed with flavor.

Pastel

Thin, crispy fried pastries stuffed with savory fillings like cheese, meat, or heart of palm. A popular Brazilian snack enjoyed anytime.

Brigadeiro

Decadent chocolate truffles made with condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter. A sweet treat loved at Brazilian celebrations and parties.

Quindim

A bright yellow, glossy coconut dessert made with egg yolks and sugar. Sweet, rich, and melt-in-your-mouth delicious.
