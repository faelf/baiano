About Us

At Baiano Restaurant, we bring the vibrant flavours and rich culture of Bahia straight to the heart of London. Our passion is serving authentic Bahian dishes made with love and tradition, using fresh ingredients and time-honoured recipes passed down through generations.
Whether you are a Bahian far from home or simply curious to explore Brazil’s unique cuisine, Baiano offers a welcoming space where every meal feels like a celebration. Join us to experience the warmth, spice, and soul of Bahia in every bite.
